How the practice works

Move through five speaking exercises modelled on the Versant format. Each activity has a timer, and your responses are recorded via the browser’s speech recognition. You can re-record when available.

  • Use headphones and a quiet space for best results.
  • Grant microphone access when prompted.
  • All data stays in your browser and clears automatically on restart.

If speech recognition is unsupported, text inputs will appear so you can still complete the exercises manually.
